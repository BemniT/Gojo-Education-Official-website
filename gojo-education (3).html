About Gojo

One platform for
the entire school

Gojo was built to solve a problem both founders experienced firsthand: Ethiopian schools are still running on paper registers, printed textbooks, and disconnected manual systems — while the rest of the world has moved to integrated digital platforms.

What started as a simple student exam-practice app evolved into a full school operating system. Gojo connects every major department and every stakeholder — students, parents, teachers, academic staff, HR, finance, and registration — into one unified cloud platform.

Gojo operates as a SaaS business. Schools subscribe monthly or annually and get immediate access to all seven modules — no hardware required, no IT team needed, up and running in minutes.

🏆
Pilot completed at Gudda Miju-PARC School in Adama, Ethiopia. The school formally recommended Gojo for scaling to other institutions through the Adama Administrative Town Education Office.

Real-world validation

Officially recommended after a real-school pilot

Gojo was piloted at a real school in Adama, Ethiopia — implemented by the development team and evaluated by school staff on actual classroom usage, teacher feedback, and student engagement.

Following the pilot period, the school issued a formal recommendation letter to the Adama Administrative Town Bole Sub-city Education Office, supporting the scaling of Gojo to other institutions.
